Guide · Use-cases

Talk to the market.

Quant72 turns plain-English questions into on-chain answers. Here's how to drive it — tap any prompt to run it live in the Terminal.

Use-cases
▤ Price & charts ◊ Price forecast ↗ Trending radar ◈ Narratives ✦ Smart mentions ◎ Rug analysis ◇ Portfolio
USE-CASE 01

Check a token's price & chart

Ask for any Solana token and Quant72 runs FETCH_PRICE, returning the live price, 24h move, volume and a candlestick chart you can scan in seconds.

Try asking
›_Show me the price and chart for SOLrun ↗ ›_How much is WIF trading at?run ↗ ›_Price chart for JUPrun ↗
returns FETCH_PRICE
Live price, 24h change & spread
Candlestick chart with timeframe toggle
Market cap, 24h volume, high / low
A sharp one-line read on the structure
